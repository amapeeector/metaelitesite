Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How long does verification take?
▼
Typically, verification takes between 3-7 days depending on your account status and the platform. Our Elite plan guarantees 3-day delivery. Peak times may vary slightly.
What are the requirements for verification?
▼
You need an active account with authentic identity information, a profile picture, at least 5,000 followers (for Instagram), and a complete bio. Different platforms have slightly different requirements.
What if my verification fails?
▼
We offer a full refund guarantee if verification fails on our end. We'll help you troubleshoot and reapply at no additional cost. Your success is our priority.
Is my account information safe?
▼
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never store sensitive information. Your data is protected by the highest security standards and only used for verification.
Do you offer customer support?
▼
Yes! We offer 24/7 support via WhatsApp, email, and Telegram. Our response time is typically under 1 hour. Premium plans include dedicated support agents.
Can you recover deleted accounts?
▼
Our account recovery service can help you regain access to disabled or deleted accounts. Success depends on the account history and reason for deletion. Contact support for a consultation.
